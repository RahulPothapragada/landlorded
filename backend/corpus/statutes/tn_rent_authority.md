# Tamil Nadu Rent Authority
## Jurisdiction, contact, and forms guide from tenancy.tn.gov.in

This note is based on the official Tamil Nadu tenancy portal.

The tenancy portal provides:

- Rent Authority

- User Manual

## How tenancy registration works on the portal
The portal FAQ states that:
1. the application for registration is made online at tenancy.tn.gov.in
2. after scrutiny, the Rent Authority notifies the applicant to present the tenancy agreement with supporting documents for verification
3. after verification, the Rent Authority issues the registration certificate

## Documents listed in the FAQ for presentation before the Rent Authority
- Original executed tenancy agreement
- Copies of title document / patta / legal heirship certificate
- Original ID proof as submitted in the online application
- Document empowering the agent to act on behalf of the principal, such as Power of Attorney, Board Resolution, Property Management Agreement, etc.

## Fee note shown in the FAQ
The portal FAQ states:
- no fee is prescribed for registration of the tenancy agreement
- a service charge of Rs.100 is levied by the service provider for online registration service

## Forms relevant to Rent Authority / Rent Court / Rent Tribunal
From the Rules published on the portal:

- Form I: website update details after registration / termination update
- Form II: intimation of expiry or earlier termination
- Form III: intimation of sub-tenancy
- Form IV: application before Rent Authority
- Form V: application before Rent Court
- Form VI: appeal before Rent Court
- Form VII: appeal before Rent Tribunal

The Kancheepuram jurisdiction PDF lists four divisions:

- Chengalpattu

For Chengalpattu Division, the appointed Rent Authority is:
- **Officer:** Revenue Divisional Officer, Chengalpattu
- **Contact:** 9445000414
- **Taluks covered:** Chengalpattu, Thirupporur, Thirukkalukundram

**Caution:** The contact-details PDF shows 9445000414 for both Chengalpattu and Madurantagam divisions — this may reflect a shared office number or PDF extraction artifact. Verify by phone before filing.

**Practical note for Estancia / Vallancheri users:** Do not assume Chennai jurisdiction despite being Chennai-metro adjacent. Open the Kancheepuram row on the portal and use Chengalpattu Division / RDO Chengalpattu for filing and contact routing.

## How to use jurisdiction PDFs
The portal links a district-specific jurisdiction PDF for each Rent Authority entry. Use that PDF to confirm:
- which taluk falls under which Rent Authority office
- which officer is designated for that area
- where to present documents if the portal calls for physical verification

## Best working approach
1. Use the portal Registration page to create / log into the account.
2. Pick the correct district and taluk.
3. Use the district Rent Authority page links to confirm jurisdiction and contact.
4. Keep Form II / Form IV / supporting documents ready.
5. Check Notifications for any newer circular, rule amendment, or procedural change before filing.
